## Impossible Pie - Greg Harano

* 4 eggs
* 1/2 c. Bisquick
* 1/2 c. sugar
* 3 T. butter
* 2 c. milk
* 1 t. vanilla
* Dash nutmeg
* Dash of salt
* 1/2 c. coconut

Combine all ingredients in blender for 10 seconds. Pour batter into 9 inch pie plate. Bake at 400 for 30 to 40 minutes.
